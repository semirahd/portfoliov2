About Semirah

Semirah makes waves in the tech industry as a founding partner of Noire Tech LLC, engineer (cloud arch. and blockchain), speaker and inventor. At the beginning of her career, Semirah got her start as a chemical engineer apprentice with the US Navy. She then went on to build her engineering arsenal tool kit across various industries. She went on to manufacturing engineering at Johnson & Johnson [neuron-surgery business unit], before becoming a mechanical engineer at Alcoa [aerospace heat treatment plant]; followed by serving as software engineer at eBay and an additional stint at eBay as a product manager. Semirah has spoken at conferences all around the world; from the Mozilla London All Hands, Grace Hopper Celebration for Women in Computing, National Society of Black Engineers, University of Massachusetts, BITCON and more! Her workshop topics span from Blockchain tech demos/introductions, Professional Development topics that have been intrinsic in her career journey and panel appearances for various topics.

During Semirah's time at eBay, she developed and patented a virtual reality experienced to aid in the enablement of live events being more handicap accessible. Semirah's exploration into the Blockchain world started in 2017 after receiving a scholarship to a blockchain developers bootcamp in San Francisco, CA. She then went on to place 2nd in an International Blockchain hackathon. These experiences were pivotal to her success and the catalyst to her business.

Real Estate Blockchain Application

Noire Tech LLC worked with Front End Engineers at a Zillow Competitor to create a tracking system for properties. The goal was to replicate the Registry of Deeds database but in a platform that was open and decentralized. This project is not in the public domain yet.

Food Supply Chain Blockchain Application

Noire Tech LLC was commissioned to provide assistance to a Walmart blockchain decentralized application project that required transparaency of their wheat/gluten related suppliers. This project will also be used for other supply chain databases within the Walmart ecosytem.

Large Event Virtual Reality/Metaverse Project

Noire Tech LLC worked with Stubhub, an event ticket selling platform, to create a Metaverse-like 3D experience for event attendees. This project is under patent review.

Video Game Wearable NFTs

Noire Tech LLC worked with a collection of small artist to design and mint video game wearable NFTs on Decentraland, Polygon, OpenSea and Raribles.

Cryptocurrency Smart Contracts ERC-20 & ERC-721

We built out a highly secure business solution that followed the clients requirements needed for a Non-fungible and unique token offering. The smart contract included role-based permissions, ledger hardware wallet support and an embeddable web3 checkout experience.

Government Projects (US Navy/Army)

Noire Tech LLC is being hired as a consultant on blockchain projects related to US government supply chain and additive manufacturing use cases.
